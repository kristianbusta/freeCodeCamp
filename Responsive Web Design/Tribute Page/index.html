Ada Lovelace's Timeline

  • 1815 | Born on December 10th in London, United Kingdom
  • 1833 | Ada met Charles Babbage, inventor of the first computer
  • 1842 - 1843 | Ada wrote the first computer program, an algorithm designed to be executed by Charles Babbage's Analytical Engine
  • 1852 | Ada died of uterine cancer on November 27th at the age of 36
